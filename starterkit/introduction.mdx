StarterKit is a collection of over 300 TypeScript starter kits for Astro and Next.js with built-in authentication, Stripe payments, and support for multiple databases and frameworks, enabling you to launch quickly with your favorite tech.

To download a TypeScript starter kit, simply [sign up](https://understated.dev/sign-up) for a StarterKit account. After a one-off purchase of StarterKit, you'll be able to choose from over 300 starter kits to build your next project.
